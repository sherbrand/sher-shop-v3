- Eyebrow: What is a Corset Top?
- H2: Structured Boning That Commands the Silhouette
- Paragraph: A corset top is a structured top built on boning. Thin, firm bones run through the fabric and hold the shape in place. It pulls in the waist and lifts the bust, so the line stays clean. Unlike a soft top, a corset top keeps its form all night.

- H2: Each Bone Set by Hand, Not by Machine
- Paragraph: We set each bone by hand on a mannequin, not laid flat by machine. That way each bone follows the curve of a real body. The fabric is cut to match, so the top holds you where it should. This is slow work, and you can see it in the fit.

- H2: Frequently Asked Questions
- H3#1: How do I measure for a corset top?
- Paragraph#1: Measure your bust, waist, hip, and length with a soft tape. Bust matters most for a corset top, so take it with care. Keep the tape level and snug, not tight. Then match your numbers to our size chart and pick that size.
- H3#2: How should a corset top fit?
- Paragraph#2: A corset top should feel firm, but never painful. It holds you in and stays put when you move. There should be no gap at the top edge or at the waist. If it pinches or you cannot breathe easy, size up.
- H3#3: What if I am between sizes?
- Paragraph#3: If you fall between two sizes, size up. A top can be taken in, but it cannot be let out. With the Lace Closure you can cinch it tighter yourself, so a touch of room is easy to fix. The larger size gives you more ways to adjust.
- H3#4: How do I care for my corset top?
- Paragraph#4: Hand wash your corset top in cool water, or have it dry cleaned. Do not machine wash, since the heat and spin can bend the boning. Lay it flat to dry, away from the sun. Always follow the care label sewn inside, as it comes first.
- H3#5: How do I style a corset top?
- Paragraph#5: Let the corset top lead. Keep the rest simple, so the eye goes to it. Pair it with plain trousers, a straight skirt, or clean denim. Add one piece of fine jewellery and stop there. When the top is the centrepiece, less around it looks like more.
- H3#6: Should I choose a Lace or Zip Closure?
- Paragraph#6: Both closures give the same fit, so choose by how you like to dress. Pick the Lace Closure if you want to adjust the shape and forgive a big meal. Pick the Zip Closure if you want to slip it on fast with nothing to set. There is no wrong choice.
