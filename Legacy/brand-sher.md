# Brand Overview

## 1. Our Brand

### Target User

List any: common characteristics ("urban professionals, 30–45"), hopes/fears in life ("to feel ahead of the curve"), pain points ("mass-market options feel generic").

- `Common` - Style-aware women 22–35, active on Instagram, who take their appearance seriously
- `Hope/Fear` - To dress in a way that shows off their body and have it read as elegant rather than cheap
- `Pain` - Premium corset tops are scarce in her market, and fast-fashion alternatives look and feel cheap

### Selling Point

List any: reasons-to-believe (why the product is better — "made by hand, not machine") or emotions-to-believe (feelings from the product — "a sense of competence").

- `Reason` - Each bone hand-set on a mannequin — structured corsetry shaped by hand, not laid flat by machine
- `Reason` - Three closure types — adjustability (Lace-up), ease (Zip Closure), or both (Lace & Zip)
- `Emotion` - Bold confidence — sensual without looking cheap, magnetic without trying

### Brand Identity

List any: reason-to-exist ("making X accessible for all"), personality adjectives ("bold, playful"), famous brand/person ("Nike, Adidas, Rihanna").

- `Vision` - SHER is a modern womenswear brand built around handcrafted corset tops, empowering women with sensuality that elevates rather than cheapens — each bone hand-set on a mannequin for a refined silhouette.
- `Adjective` - Magnetic, Sensual, Feminine, Refined
- `Brand/Person` - Cherie (cherieattire.com — Sweet Sensuality), Perfect Corset (perfectcorsetny.com — Bold Sensuality), Gigi Hadid (Off-Duty Model)
- `Voice` - Direct, assured, quietly seductive. Never promotional

## 3. What We Sell

### Categories

List product categories and their sub-categories, e.g., "Apparel — Dresses, Tops, Outerwear". For software or service brands, relabel as Features / Sub-features.

- `Corset Tops` - The flagship category; structured pieces built on corsetry construction
  - `Lace-up` - Eyelets and cord; adjustable fit, total control
  - `Zip Closure` - Separating zip; easy to wear
  - `Lace & Zip` - The fit of lace and the ease of a zip
- `Matching Sets` - Two pieces that match — a top with a skirt or trousers
  - `Skirt Set` - A matching top and skirt
  - `Trouser Set` - A matching top and trousers

### Key Terms (optional)

List any: product attributes ("book binding: layflat, canvas finishing: framed"), technical terms ("hand-set boning, satin lining"). If none apply, write "None yet."

- `hand-set boning` - Bones individually positioned on a mannequin, not machine-laid flat
- `boning channel` - Inner fabric channel that holds each bone in place
- `back lacing` - Cord laced through eyelets at the back of the garment; adjustable
- `eyelet` - Metal-rimmed hole that the lacing cord passes through
- `separating zip` - Two-part zipper that opens fully so the garment comes apart for wear
- `satin lining` - Inner satin layer for comfort and shape retention
- `structured bust cups` - Pre-shaped bust panels built into the garment
