# SHER — Claude Design Input

## The brand

SHER is a premium womenswear brand built on handcrafted corset tops — structured corsetry shaped by hand, sensual without looking cheap. She is style-aware, 22–35, takes her appearance seriously, and wants to read as elegant, never cheap. Personality: magnetic, sensual, feminine, refined.

## The world

Editorial fashion photography: structured corsetry in satin, a palette of nude, black, and white, against marble, staircases, and architectural light — with an off-duty-model ease. Reference points: Cherie (sweet sensuality), Perfect Corset (bold sensuality), Gigi Hadid (off-duty model). Photography is the brand; everything else frames it.

## Voice

Direct, assured, quietly seductive. Never promotional.

## Palette — fixed

Warm neutrals only. Never introduce cool or blue-toned greys, gradients, or colours outside this set.

- background `#F2F2EC` — warm off-white base
- surface `#E3E2DD` — alternate section background
- primary `#A99D94` — sandy grey; the only colour for interactive elements
- dark `#504F4D` — charcoal; dark sections, footer, high-contrast surfaces
- text `#665D55` — body text and headings
- muted `#929290` — non-interactive icons, dividers, metadata
- white `#FFFFFF` — on dark surfaces

## Type — fixed identity, flexible sizing

Three typefaces, each with a fixed role — never substitute:

- **Cormorant Infant** — display, always uppercase, fashion-forward. Headings only.
- **Cardo** — editorial serif. Product copy: names, prices, descriptions.
- **Manrope** — functional sans. UI chrome: navigation, labels, buttons.

Display type is large and confident; functional type recedes. Size the scale yourself.

## Feel — hold to these

- Image-led: let photography fill its space. Never crop, pad, or box-in an image, and never let UI compete with the hero.
- Full-bleed imagery for hero and lookbook.
- Flat and photographic: depth comes from imagery and tonal contrast, not heavy shadows.
- Restrained, unhurried motion — nothing bounces, springs, or overshoots.
- Soft, small corner radius on interactive elements. Refined, not playful.

## Open — propose these

Spacing rhythm, section depth, component styling, and motion detail are intentionally left open. Generate them on-brand and we'll react.

## Avoid

Generic AI-landing-page tells: cool blue-greys, heavy drop shadows, bouncy or springy motion, pill-shaped everything, bright gradients, and placeholder-sounding copy. SHER is the opposite of a generic SaaS page.
