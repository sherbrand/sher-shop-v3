- H2: Frequently Asked Questions
- H3#1: How do I measure for a matching set?
- Paragraph#1: Measure your bust, waist, hip, and the length of each piece. For a matching set the waist matters most, since it shapes both the top and the bottom. Keep the tape level and snug. Match your numbers to our size chart and pick that size.
- H3#2: How should a matching set fit?
- Paragraph#2: The top should feel firm and stay put, the way a corset top does. The bottom sits at your natural waist, though the exact spot can shift by style. When you stand, the two hems should meet without a gap. If they do not, check the size chart again.
- H3#3: What if I am between sizes?
- Paragraph#3: If you fall between two sizes, size up the top. A structured top can be taken in, but it cannot be let out. For the bottom, follow the size chart for your waist. Sizing up the top gives you room to adjust later.
- H3#4: How do I care for my matching set?
- Paragraph#4: Care depends on the fabric, so read the care label first. Some sets hand wash, some machine wash cool, and some need dry cleaning. Wash both pieces together, so they fade at the same rate and stay matched. Lay them flat to dry, away from the sun.
- H3#5: How do I style a matching set?
- Paragraph#5: Wear both pieces together, since that is the point of a set. The match does the work, so keep everything else simple. Add one bag and one pair of shoes in a plain tone. Skip loud jewellery and let the set carry the look on its own.
- H3#6: Can I buy the top and bottom on their own?
- Paragraph#6: A matching set is sold only as a pair. We design the two pieces to be worn together, so we keep them together. This makes sure the fabric and colour always match. If you want a single piece, browse our other tops and bottoms instead.
